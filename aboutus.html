How it all started

Icecaps was created to faciliate entry to cryptocurrency market for individuals that prefer professional asset management.

Nicholas Lexa and Matthew Vondrak founded Icecaps due to strong passion for digital assets. Having rich experience gathered through several years helped them to focus on important aspects of the crypto space. This all blended into several strategies being utilized at Icecaps, each being leveraged to present best possible outcomes for individual clients.
