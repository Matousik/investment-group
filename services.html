Services & Products

At Icecaps, we are conviced that digital assets can generate significant returns with associated high risk. Therefore we offer an intelligent exposure to all spheres of cryptocurrency investing opportunities in two distinct products.

Our clientele is composed of various high net worth institutional or individual investors seeking sustainable longterm exposure to digital assets with flexible risk management. Investment strategy, a vital component of sustainable profitable investing, is created individually for each client.

It doesn't matter if you're seeking high risk investments or prefer safer approach mainly focused on wealth conservation. Together we can create a perfect investment strategy for your exposure with optimal risk levels, with highest possible returns.

Digital asset management

The main product offered by Icecaps. Complex management of centralized exchange account.

Stablecoin staking

Very low risk side product consisting of proper stablecoin staking allocation, focused mainly on wealth conservation.
